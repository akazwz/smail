## About smail.pw

smail.pw is a simple tool for creating disposable email addresses in seconds. It helps you sign up for new services, receive one-off verification codes, and download resources without exposing your real inbox.

### Why smail.pw exists

The modern web asks for your email almost everywhere: to read an article, try a product, or download a file. Over time this leads to:

- Inbox clutter from newsletters and promos you did not really want
- More tracking of your online activity across services
- A larger blast radius if a site suffers a data breach

smail.pw gives you a lightweight alternative. Instead of handing out your primary address, you can:

- Use a temporary address for low-risk sign-ups
- Receive the email or code you need
- Walk away once you are done

### How it works

1. Open smail.pw.
2. Use the generated temporary email address where you need it.
3. Check this page to see incoming messages to that address.
4. Copy out any important information you want to keep.

Inboxes and emails are temporary by design. They may be deleted at any time and should not be treated as long-term storage.

### What smail.pw is and is not

smail.pw **is**:

- A quick way to receive temporary emails
- Useful for trials, low-risk registrations, and one-off downloads
- A way to keep marketing and automated messages away from your real inbox

smail.pw is **not**:

- A replacement for a real, long-term email account
- Suitable for banking, work, government, or legal communications
- A secure archive for important or sensitive data

If you need guarantees about delivery, security, or long-term access, you should always use a trusted personal email provider instead.

### Our approach

smail.pw aims to stay small, predictable, and focused on one thing: giving you a disposable inbox with as little friction as possible.
