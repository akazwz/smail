## Online Temporary Email

If you need email reception in the browser right now, **online temporary email** gives you an instant inbox for OTP and verification links.

### Why users search online temp mail

- Immediate access without installing apps
- Quick inbox refresh for live verification flows
- Better separation between disposable and personal addresses

### Usage tips

- Copy the address exactly to avoid delivery errors.
- If the email does not appear, resend from the source platform.
- Wait a short moment and refresh again because sender queues can delay delivery.

### Retention

Messages are kept for up to 24 hours and then removed automatically.
