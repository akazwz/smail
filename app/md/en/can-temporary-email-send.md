## Can Temporary Email Send Messages?

Many users ask whether a temporary mailbox can send outbound email. In most cases, **temporary email is receive-only** and designed for OTP and confirmation intake.

### Why receive-only is common

- Lower abuse risk (spam, fraud, phishing)
- Simpler anti-abuse and moderation controls
- Faster infrastructure for disposable inbox usage

### When to use a permanent mailbox instead

Use a long-term provider if you need:

- Sending and replying to messages
- Account ownership and identity continuity
- Long-term archives and recovery workflows

### Conclusion

Use smail.pw for receiving verification messages quickly. Use a permanent email service when sending is required.
